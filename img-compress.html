Frequently Asked Questions

How does image compression work?

Image compression reduces the file size of an image by removing unnecessary data while maintaining visual quality as much as possible.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats for compression.
